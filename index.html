Nike Pegasus Evolution

(2016–2024)

Nike Pegasus 33 (2016):
Praised for its added forefoot Zoom Air unit, runners loved the responsive cushioning and smooth transitions.

Nike Pegasus 34 (2017):
Popular for its breathable upper and consistent ride, it became a go-to for everyday training.

Nike Pegasus 35 (2018):
Introduced a full-length Zoom Air unit, providing a sleeker, faster feel with great comfort.

Nike Pegasus 36 (2019):
Loved for its slimmer tongue and collar, offering a more streamlined fit while maintaining reliable cushioning.

Nike Pegasus 37 (2020):
The shift to React foam and a thicker Zoom Air bag gave this version a softer, springier feel, which some found transformative.

Nike Pegasus 38 (2021):
Maintained the React foam but improved upper comfort, with many appreciating its wide-foot-friendly fit.

Nike Pegasus 39 (2022):
Lightweight yet cushioned, this model impressed runners with its combination of breathability and responsiveness.

Nike Pegasus 40 (2023):
Celebrated for its balance of durability and comfort, it became a solid choice for both beginners and seasoned runners.

Nike Pegasus 41 (2024):
Known for its improved traction and refined fit, it continues to be a favorite for versatile training needs.
